About us

Who we are, what we do, and why we do it.

Assembler Studio

Assembler Studio is the creative and digital branch of Assembler Computer, a long-standing IT lab based in Cuneo (Italy), established by Davy many years ago.

We design and build tailor-made websites, helping companies, professionals and organizations showcase their identity with clarity, impact, and visual coherence.

Our approach is practical, human-centered and tech-driven: we deliver modern, fast, secure static websites, created with care and long-term vision.

Our founder Manuel is an experienced IT consultant who collaborates with Assembler Computer, offering technical support, systems configuration and infrastructure design for small businesses, freelancers and local enterprises.
