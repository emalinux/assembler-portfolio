Palawan Tribe

A non-profit initiative to give visibility and support to the Palawan tribal communities. Built with love by Manuel – Assembler Computer.

🧭 Description

Official website of Palawan Tribe Dolce Amore, a non-profit association supporting indigenous communities in Palawan, Philippines.

Built with ❤️ by Manuel – Assembler Computer, the site is multilingual (Italian & English), fast, sustainable, and designed to reflect the warmth and harmony of tribal life.

Includes sections on history, vision, photo gallery, and contact – all optimized for mobile and slow connections, with ethical SEO and zero tracking.

🌐 Visit the website →

🚀 Technologies

  • Hugo (static site generator)
  • Custom HTML/CSS for a warm and immersive visual experience
  • Netlify for free continuous deployment and hosting
  • Native multilingual structure with separated content folders (IT / EN)
  • Ethical SEO: optimized indexing without ads or tracking
