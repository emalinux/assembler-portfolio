Agriturismo - La Ciapeleta

Official website of Agriturismo Ciapéleta, built as a modern, fast, and SEO-oriented static site.

🏡 Description

Official website of La Ciapéleta Farmhouse, a wonderful agriturismo with panoramic sea views in Ventimiglia (IM), Italy.

Built with Hugo and custom HTML/CSS, the site is lightweight, fast, and user-friendly — perfect even for hillside areas with limited connectivity. Each section was designed to reflect the authentic atmosphere of the property, using warm visuals and clear, welcoming text.

It features pages about rooms, restaurant, contact info, and booking.
The design is minimalist yet cozy, aligned with the visual identity of the farmhouse.

🚀 Technologies

  • Hugo (static site generator)
  • Custom HTML/CSS for a modern and accessible look
  • Netlify for fast and reliable deployment
  • On-page SEO for better Google indexing
  • Lightweight design optimized for slow connections (countryside vibes!)
