TB Logistica S.r.l.

Showcase website for a logistics and transport company. Responsive design, clean layout, SEO-oriented.

🚛 Description

Official showcase website of TB Logistica S.r.l., a company operating in the field of transport and logistics across Italy.

The project was built using Hugo as a high-performance static site generator, with the goal of conveying reliability, corporate strength, and clarity of services.
It features a clean, professional, and fully responsive layout, designed for seamless browsing on both desktop and mobile.

The content structure includes company presentation, offered services, contact information, and key operational details—crafted with maximum readability and SEO optimization.
